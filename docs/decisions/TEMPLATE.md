# NNNN — Short title in imperative form

**Status**: Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by NNNN
**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the situation that requires a decision? What constraints, prior work, or external factors are relevant? Keep this factual — describe the problem, not the solution.

## Decision

What did we choose to do? State it plainly in one paragraph.

## Alternatives considered

- **Alternative A** — why it was rejected
- **Alternative B** — why it was rejected

## Consequences

- **Positive**: what gets easier, more correct, or possible
- **Negative**: what gets harder, what we're committing to maintain
- **Neutral**: tradeoffs that aren't clearly wins or losses

## References

Links to research, PRs, code, or external docs that informed the decision.
